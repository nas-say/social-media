## üìñ Part 1: Understanding Templates (Why Bother?)

### What is a Template?

Think of it like a Microsoft Word letterhead you use for all your emails:
- Logo in the corner ‚úÖ
- Your signature at bottom ‚úÖ
- Same font/colors every time ‚úÖ

**Without template**: You format every email from scratch (10 min each)  
**With template**: You open template, type message, send (1 min)

### Why Templates for Reels?

**Without template** (doing it from scratch every time):
1. Open Canva ‚Üí New design
2. Choose size (1080x1920)
3. Pick background color
4. Add text box
5. Choose font... Bebas Neue, 80pt, white
6. Add stroke effect (black, 3px)
7. Position text at top
8. Add video
9. Add vignette effect
10. Adjust opacity
11. Export

**Time: 8-10 minutes per reel √ó 21 reels = 3 hours**

---

**WITH template** (you do setup once, then reuse):
1. Open saved template
2. Duplicate page
3. Replace video
4. Replace audio
5. Change text
6. Export

**Time: 2-3 minutes per reel √ó 21 reels = 1 hour**

**You save 2 hours every single week.**

## üé® Part 2: Creating Your Master Template (One-Time Setup)

### Time: 20-30 minutes (you only do this ONCE)

---

### Step 1: Open Canva and Create New Design

**What to do:**
1. Go to: canva.com
2. Log in with your Canva Edu account
3. Click purple "Create a design" button (top right)
4. Click "Custom size"
5. Enter:
   - Width: **1080**
   - Height: **1920**
   - Unit: **px** (pixels)
6. Click "Create new design"

**You now have**: A blank vertical canvas (perfect for Instagram Reels)

**Name it**: Click "Untitled Design" at top ‚Üí Type "Story Reel Master Template"

---

### Step 2: Create Template Page 1 - Dark Mystery

**This is your base template for mysterious/dark stories**

**Layer 1: Background Color**
1. Click **Elements** (left sidebar)
2. Search: "square"
3. Click the plain square shape
4. Drag to cover entire canvas
5. Click square ‚Üí Change color to **#0A0A0A** (deep black)

**Layer 2: Video Placeholder**
1. Click **Uploads** (left sidebar)
2. Click "+ Upload files"
3. Upload ANY short video from your computer (doesn't matter what - we'll replace it later)
4. Drag video onto canvas
5. Resize to fill screen (1080 x 1920)
6. **Right-click video** ‚Üí "Set video clip as background"

**Layer 3: Dark Overlay** (makes text readable)
1. Click **Elements** ‚Üí Search "square"
2. Add square over video
3. Resize to cover screen
4. Change color to black (#000000)
5. Click square ‚Üí **Transparency** slider ‚Üí Set to **40%**
6. Now video is slightly darkened, perfect for text!

**Layer 4: Vignette Effect** (dark edges, focus center)
1. Click **Elements** ‚Üí Search "vignette"
2. Find a circular gradient that's dark on edges
3. Drag over canvas, resize to cover
4. Adjust transparency to **30-50%**

**Layer 5: Hook Text** (the catchy opening line)
1. Click **Text** (left sidebar)
2. Click "Add a heading"
3. Type: "YOUR HOOK TEXT GOES HERE"
4. **Font**: Click font dropdown ‚Üí Search "Bebas Neue" or "Impact"
5. **Size**: 80-90pt
6. **Color**: White (#FFFFFF)
7. **Position**: Top third of screen
8. **Add outline** (so text pops):
   - Click text
   - Click "Effects" (top toolbar)
   - Click "Outline"
   - Color: Black
   - Thickness: 3-4

**Layer 6: Bottom Text Area** (optional story details)
1. Add another text box
2. Font: Roboto or Open Sans, 44pt
3. Color: White
4. Position: Lower third
5. Type: "Story details here..."

**Your first template page is done!** This took 10-15 minutes, but you'll NEVER do it again.

## üé• Part 3: Understanding Pexels Videos (Stock Footage)

### What is Pexels?

**Pexels = Free stock video library**

- Thousands of professional videos
- 100% free to use (even commercially)
- No watermarks
- No attribution required
- Perfect for backgrounds

### Why Use Pexels?

Your reels are **storytelling** (voiceover explaining a story). You need:
- Visual backgrounds that match the mood
- NOT you on camera (faceless channel)
- Professional-looking footage

**Examples:**
- True crime story about unsolved murder ‚Üí Dark corridor video
- Historical story about library ‚Üí Vintage books video
- Mystery about ocean ‚Üí Stormy ocean waves video

Pexels provides all of this FOR FREE.

---

### How to Find the Right Videos on Pexels

**Step-by-step:**

1. **Go to**: pexels.com/videos
2. **Important**: Click "Orientation" filter ‚Üí Select **"Portrait"** or **"Vertical"**
   - Why? Instagram Reels are vertical (9:16)
   - You need vertical videos, not horizontal
3. **Search by mood/category**:
   - For True Crime: "dark corridor", "crime scene", "investigation"
   - For Historical: "vintage", "old documents", "sepia library"
   - For Unexplained: "mysterious fog", "dark forest", "paranormal"
   - For Survival: "ocean storm", "wilderness", "extreme weather"
4. **Click video** you like
5. **Click "Free Download"**
6. **Choose quality**: Pick "HD" or "Full HD" (1080p is perfect)
7. **Save to folder**: Save as `video-01.mp4`, `video-02.mp4`, etc.

**Organize your downloads:**
```
Desktop/
  reel-content/
    week-1/
      videos/
        video-01.mp4 (dark corridor.mp4)
        video-02.mp4 (vintage library.mp4)
        ...
      audio/
        (voiceovers go here later)
      final-reels/
        (exported reels go here)
```

---

### What Video Length Should You Download?

**Short answer**: Doesn't matter!

- Pexels videos are usually 15-60 seconds
- Your voiceover is usually 40-50 seconds
- In Canva, you can:
  - **Trim** video if too long
  - **Loop** video if too short
  - **Match to audio** automatically

**Just download vertical videos that look good. Length is flexible.**

## üéØ Part 7: Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "My text doesn't show up over the video!"

**Problem**: Video layer is on TOP of text layer  
**Fix**:
1. Right-click video ‚Üí "Set as background"
2. Or click video ‚Üí Click "Position" ‚Üí Send to back

---

### "Video is sideways/wrong size!"

**Problem**: You downloaded horizontal video, not vertical  
**Fix**:
1. Go back to Pexels
2. Filter: Orientation ‚Üí Portrait/Vertical
3. Download new video
4. Or: In Canva, click video ‚Üí "Crop" ‚Üí Rotate to vertical

---

### "Audio doesn't match video length!"

**Problem**: Video is 20 sec, audio is 50 sec  
**Fix**: Canva auto-extends video by looping. Check:
1. Click video
2. Click "Edit video"
3. Check "Loop video" is ON
4. Video will automatically repeat to match audio

---

### "Export takes forever / gets stuck!"

**Problem**: Large file size or slow internet  
**Fix**:
1. Check video quality: Use "Standard" not "4K"
2. Close other Canva tabs
3. Wait 2-3 minutes (rendering takes time)
4. If still stuck: Refresh page, try export again

---

### "Text is hard to read over video!"

**Problem**: Video too bright, or wrong text color  
**Fix**:
1. Add darker overlay (black square at 50% opacity)
2. Increase text outline thickness (4-5px instead of 3px)
3. Use white text on dark videos, dark text on light videos
4. Add text background: Click text ‚Üí "Effects" ‚Üí "Background"

---

### "I have too many pages in my template now!"

**Problem**: You duplicated pages and now have 30 pages  
**Fix**:
1. Right-click page thumbnail (bottom)
2. Click "Delete page"
3. Keep only your original 5 template pages
4. OR: Create a NEW project for each week's batch
   - Duplicate master template ‚Üí Rename "Week 1 Reels"
   - Create all 21 reels in that new project
   - Keeps your master clean
