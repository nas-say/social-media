### ‚úÖ 3. ChatGPT Organization (15 min)

**Create Saved Prompts Document:**

1. Open Google Docs or Notion
2. Create document: "ChatGPT Prompts for Storytelling"
3. Copy these essential prompts:

**Script Generator Prompt:**
```
Write a 60-second story script about [TOPIC].

Structure:
- Hook (1-2 sentences, shocking opener)
- Setup (2-3 sentences, context)
- Rising action (3-4 sentences, build tension)
- Twist (2 sentences, dramatic reveal)
- Resolution (1-2 sentences)
- Engagement CTA (question or follow)

Tone: Mysterious, compelling, factual
Word count: 150-180 words
Format for voiceover: Short sentences, dramatic pauses with "..."
```

**Hook Generator Prompt:**
```
Generate 10 hooks for a story about [TOPIC].

Each hook must:
- Be 5-10 words maximum
- Create immediate curiosity
- Use specific numbers or details
- Pattern interrupt (make them stop scrolling)

Examples:
- "In 1995, she vanished. In 2023, she returned unchanged."
- "918 people died in one day. Most didn't choose it."
```

**Story Research Prompt:**
```
I'm creating a reel about [TOPIC]. Provide:
1. Key facts and timeline (bullet points)
2. Lesser-known details that make it interesting
3. The most dramatic/shocking element
4. 2-3 reliable sources I can verify
```

**‚úÖ Done when**: Prompts are saved and easily accessible
