### Canva Setup:

**Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920px (Instagram Reel)

**Layer Structure**:

1. **Background Layer**:
   - Dark, moody footage (forest at night, foggy street, old building)
   - Apply: Darkening filter (-30% brightness)
   - Add: Film grain overlay (10% opacity)
   - Vignette: Heavy (draws eye to center)

2. **Hook Section** (0-3 seconds):
   - Text: Bebas Neue or Oswald, 90pt, White
   - Text effect: Slight glow or drop shadow
   - Position: Center or top third
   - Animation: Fade in + scale up
   - Background: Optional dark semi-transparent box

3. **Story Content** (4-55 seconds):
   - Text: Roboto or Open Sans, 40-48pt
   - Appears: Word-by-word or line-by-line
   - Spacing: 1.5x line height (readability)
   - Positioning: Lower third (leaves room for visuals)
   - Color: White with black stroke (ensures readability)

4. **Visual Elements**:
   - Stock footage changes every 5-8 seconds
   - Subtle zoom in/out (creates movement)
   - Fade transitions between clips
   - Never jarring cuts

5. **Cliffhanger Section** (56-60 seconds):
   - Text: "Part 2 tomorrow..." or "Follow for the answer"
   - Larger font: 70pt
   - Animation: Pulse or bounce
   - Optional: Arrow pointing to follow button area

### Color Grading Presets:

**Mystery/Crime**:
- Cool tones: Increase blues (+15%), decrease warmth (-10%)
- Contrast: +20%
- Saturation: -15% (desaturated look)

**Historical**:
- Sepia or warm tones
- Slight blur for "old photo" feel
- Vignette: Medium

**Survival**:
- Natural colors, high contrast
- Sharpen: +10%
- Bright highlights, deep shadows

```

**Historical Mystery**:

Accent: #FFD700 (gold)
Text: #F5F5F5 (off-white)
Secondary: #8B7355 (aged bronze)
```

**Unexplained/Paranormal**:
```
Background: #0F0F23 (midnight blue)
Accent: #9D4EDD (mysterious purple)
Text: #FFFFFF (white)
Secondary: #00FFF0 (cyan glow)
```

**Survival Stories**:
```
Background: #1C1C1C (charcoal)
Accent: #FF6B35 (survival orange)
Text: #FFFFFF (white)
Secondary: #4A7C59 (forest green)
```

### Typography Hierarchy:

**Primary Font (Headlines)**: Bebas Neue or Oswald
- Hook: 90-110pt
- Story title: 70-80pt
- Section headers: 60pt

**Secondary Font (Body)**: Roboto or Open Sans
- Main story text: 40-48pt
- Supporting details: 32-36pt
- Citations/sources: 24pt

**Accent Font (Dramatic moments)**: Cinzel or Playfair Display
- Use sparingly for impact
- Historical quotes: 44pt italics
- Final reveals: 70pt

### Animation Standards:

**Pacing for Storytelling**:
- Hook: 0.5-1 sec fade in (immediate impact)
- Story text: Appear word-by-word (builds tension)
- Speed: 0.3-0.4 seconds per word
- Dramatic pauses: 1-2 sec hold on key revelations
- Transitions: Smooth fades (0.3-0.5 sec), avoid jarring cuts

**Optimal Reel Length**: 45-60 seconds
- Shorter than AI content
- Storytelling benefits from fuller narratives
- Watch time is king

## ðŸŽµ Audio Selection Guide

### Music by Story Type:

**True Crime**:
- Dark, brooding piano
- Minimal percussion
- Tension-building strings
- Examples: "Dark Ambient" on Epidemic Sound

**Historical Mystery**:
- Orchestral
- Epic, sweeping
- Period-appropriate when possible
- Examples: "Cinematic Drama" playlists

**Unexplained Phenomena**:
- Ethereal, otherworldly
- Synthesizers, ambient sounds
- Building crescendo
- Examples: "Mystery" or "Suspense" tags

**Survival Stories**:
- Starts tense, builds to triumphant
- Drums for urgency
- Uplifting finish
- Examples: "Epic Inspirational" genre

### Volume Mixing:
- Music: 60-70% volume
- Voiceover (if used): 100% volume (primary)
- Sound effects: 40-50% volume (accent only)

**Pro Tip**: Lower music volume -3dB during voiceover/text reveals, bring back up during b-roll

## ðŸ“ Technical Specifications

### Export Settings:

**Resolution**: 1080 x 1920px (9:16 aspect ratio)
**Frame Rate**: 30fps (smooth, not too cinematic)
**Format**: MP4
**Codec**: H.264
**Bitrate**: 8-10 Mbps (balance quality/file size)
**File Size**: Under 200MB (Instagram limit)

### Text Readability Rules:

**Minimum font size**: 32pt (must be readable on phone)
**Stroke/Outline**: 2-4px black stroke on white text (or reverse)
**Contrast ratio**: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG standard)
**Safe zones**: Keep text 80px from edges
**Duration on screen**: 3+ seconds per sentence

### Mobile Preview Checklist:
- [ ] Text readable on smallest screen (iPhone SE)
- [ ] Colors look good on OLED screens
- [ ] Dark scenes aren't too dark (adjust brightness)
- [ ] Text doesn't overlap important visuals
- [ ] CTA visible in last frame
