## ðŸŽ¨ Content Pillars (The 5-Pillar Framework)

### Pillar 1: AI Tool Tutorials (40% of content)
**Purpose**: Show practical AI applications
**Examples**:
- "This ChatGPT prompt writes your emails in 30 seconds"
- "AI tool that creates presentations from a single sentence"
- "I used AI to automate my entire calendar"

**Format**: Screen recording + text overlay + voiceover (AI voice)

### Pillar 2: Productivity Hacks (30% of content)
**Purpose**: Quick wins for time management
**Examples**:
- "The 2-minute rule that changed my productivity"
- "Why successful people use time blocking"
- "3 apps that save me 10 hours per week"

**Format**: Animated text + stock footage + trending audio

### Pillar 3: Before/After Results (15% of content)
**Purpose**: Social proof, aspirational content
**Examples**:
- "My workflow before vs after using AI"
- "How I went from 60-hour weeks to 30-hour weeks"
- "This tool turned my chaotic notes into organized tasks"

**Format**: Split screen comparisons with dramatic difference

### Pillar 4: Tool Recommendations & Reviews (10% of content)
**Purpose**: Build trust, affiliate opportunities
**Examples**:
- "Top 5 AI tools for content creators"
- "Free alternatives to expensive productivity apps"
- "Is ChatGPT Plus worth $20/month? Honest review"

**Format**: List-based content with quick demos

### Pillar 5: Engaging/Viral Content (5% of content)
**Purpose**: Algorithm boost, reach new audiences
**Examples**:
- "Things I wish I knew before spending $1000 on productivity apps"
- "Green flags of a productive person"
- "POV: You just discovered AI automation"

**Format**: Trend-jacking with niche twist

## ðŸš€ Growth Tactics

### Content Optimization
1. **Hook Formula** (First 1 second):
   - Use pattern interrupts: "Stop doing X..."
   - Ask provocative questions: "Why do successful people..."
   - Make bold claims: "This AI tool is better than..."

2. **Retention Tactics**:
   - Text appears word-by-word (keeps watching)
   - Progress bars for lists
   - "Wait for #3" teasers
   - Loop-worthy endings

3. **CTA Strategy**:
   - Every 3rd reel: "Follow for more AI tips"
   - Every 5th reel: "Link in bio for free guide"
   - Test different CTAs, track what converts

### Hashtag Strategy
**Primary (Always use)**:
- #AItools #productivitytips #ChatGPT #AIhacks #worksmarter

**Secondary (Rotate)**:
- #remotework #entrepreneurtips #timemanagement #automation #techtools

**Niche-specific (Based on content)**:
- For Notion: #notiontemplate #notionsetup
- For AI: #artificialintelligence #machinelearning
- For productivity: #productivityhacks #gtd

**Format**: 8-12 hashtags per post, mix of large (1M+), medium (100k-1M), and small (10k-100k)

### Engagement Tactics (15 min/day)
1. **Before posting** (5 min):
   - Comment on 5 posts in your niche
   - Meaningful comments, not generic "Great post!"

2. **After posting** (10 min):
   - Reply to ALL comments within first hour (algorithm boost)
   - Ask questions in replies to spark conversation
   - Pin best comment to top

3. **Weekly** (30 min):
   - DM 10 complementary creators for collaboration
   - Engage with followers' content
   - Post story with poll/question sticker

### Collaboration Strategy
**Target creators**:
- 5k-50k followers (more likely to respond)
- Complementary niches: entrepreneurship, side hustles, tech
- Similar engagement rates (10%+)

**Collaboration types**:
- Shared content challenges
- Cross-promotion in stories
- Guest content swaps
- Joint giveaways (digital products)
