## üí∞ Revenue Philosophy

**Core Principle**: Monetize through value creation, not exploitation.

Your audience trusts you for productivity solutions. Every product/service should:
- ‚úÖ Actually save time or improve results
- ‚úÖ Be something you'd use yourself
- ‚úÖ Solve a real problem

## üéØ The 7 Revenue Streams

### Stream 1: Affiliate Marketing üí≥
**Start**: Day 1  
**Effort**: Low  
**Revenue Potential**: $100-$1,000/month  
**Scalability**: Medium

#### Tools to Promote:
1. **ChatGPT Plus** ($20/month)
   - Affiliate: OpenAI Partner Program (if accepted) or review-based indirect
   - Commission: No direct program, but drives traffic to your products
   - Strategy: Create comparison content, prompt guides

2. **Notion** (Free + Paid plans)
   - Affiliate: Notion Partner Program
   - Commission: $5-10 per signup
   - Strategy: Template tours, integration tutorials

3. **Canva Pro** ($12.99/month)
   - Affiliate: Canva Affiliate Program
   - Commission: 30-day cookies, recurring revenue
   - Strategy: Design tutorials, template showcases

4. **Productivity Tools**:
   - **Todoist**: Task management
   - **Otter.ai**: Transcription
   - **Grammarly**: Writing assistance
   - **Calendly**: Scheduling
   
5. **AppSumo** (Software deals)
   - Affiliate: AppSumo Partner Program
   - Commission: 15% on purchases
   - Strategy: Deal alerts, tool reviews

#### Implementation:
```
Week 1: 
- Sign up for all affiliate programs
- Set up affiliate link tracking (Bitly or Taplio)
- Add "Tools I Use" link to bio

Week 2:
- Create comparison content featuring affiliate tools
- Add affiliate links in 50% of relevant posts
- Disclose partnerships (FTC compliance)

Month 2 onwards:
- Track which tools convert best
- Double down on high-performers
- Request custom discount codes for followers
```

#### Expected Revenue:
- Month 1: $10-30 (first commissions)
- Month 3: $100-200
- Month 6: $400-800
- Month 12: $800-1,500/month
